Overview

This session provides an overview of the publishing process for iOS, Android and Windows. It concentrates on the preparation steps you should consider and perform before you push your application onto the various stores.

Objectives

  1. Getting ready to publish your app
  2. Understanding publishing styles
  3. Publishing to a store

Lecture

Follow on lectures

This class is intended to prepare you for the publishing process. However, the mechanics of each platform's marketplace/store is not covered here, and is instead in the following recorded presentations. We recommend you watch the specific video for the platform you are interested in publishing.
